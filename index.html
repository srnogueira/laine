About laine |

laine can solve nonlinear systems of equations in a easy way. Just input your equations and press Solve (F2) or Report (F4):

$$\text{In math: }x^2+x=3$$ $$\text{In laine: x^2+x=3}$$

You can also use Functions to input thermodynamic properties in your problem. For example, the specific enthalpy of Air at 298 K and 1E5 Pa can be written in laine as:

$$h_{air} = \text{PropsSI('H','T',298,'P',1E5,'Air')}$$

laine can also create a Parametric Analysis or a Property Plot to visualize your results. Just remember that a Parametric Analysis requires a problem with just one (1) degree of freedom, while a Property Plot requires that the problem calls the function PropsSI.

If you need more details you can consult the Documentation. You can also report an issue on our Github page.
