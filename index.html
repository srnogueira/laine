How to use?

Write equations in the box below and click in Solve or press F2.

The equations have to follow the structure left = right.

The of equations number has to be equal to the number of variables.

You can also include a comment by using a hashtag (#) in the beginning of the sentece.

How to handle errors?

Check if the equations are typed correctly.

Check if the problem is feasible.

Simplify equations and avoid inversions (e.g. x=1/y).

Please contact me if you could not solve your problem.
